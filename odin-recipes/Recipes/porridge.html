Tasty, Creamy Porridge

This easy porridge recipe is bound to set you up for the day.

Ingredients

  • 110g Oats
  • 250ml Whole Milk
  • 10g Chia Seeds
  • 100g Yogurt
  • 40g Almonds
  • 1 Kiwi
  • A sprinkle of Coconut Flakes

Steps

  1. First, you put the oats, milk and chia seeds into a bowl, giving it a slight mix.
  2. Then, you put the bowl into the microwave for 3 minutes.
  3. While it is being cooked in the microwave, cut a kiwi into tiny pieces.
  4. Once it is finished in the microwave, take it out and but on yogurt, almonds, your chopped kiwi and a sprinkle of coconut flakes.
  5. Get out a spoon and enjoy!
