Soft, Chewy Oat Cookies

These oat cookies are super simple to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 Bananas
  • 1 Egg
  • 180g Oats
  • 20ml Milk
  • 10g Honey

Steps

  1. First, you mash up the 2 bananas in a bowl.
  2. Then, crack the egg into the bowl.
  3. MIx the banana and egg together.
  4. Then add in the milk, honey and oats.
  5. Stir them all together.
  6. Make them into circles on a lined baking tray.
  7. Bake them for 12-14 minutes.
  8. Enjoy!
