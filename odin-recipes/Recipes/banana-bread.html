Banana Bread

Home

Soft, chewy banana bread.

This banana bread can last for about 1.5 to 2 weeks in the fridge, if it isn't gone by then!

.

Ingredients

  • 3 Bananas
  • 1 Egg
  • 180g Oat Flour
  • 30ml Milk
  • 20g Honey
  • 15g Walnuts

Steps

  1. First you mash 3 banana in a bowl,
  2. After they are mashed, add in the egg and mix.
  3. Add in the oat flour, milk and honey and mix.
  4. Put it into a lined baking dish and put the walnuts on top.
  5. Put it into the oven for 40-45 minutes.
  6. Place on a rack and let cool for 45 minutes.
  7. Put it into a container in the fridge to keep it for longer.
